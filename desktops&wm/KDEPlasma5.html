Introduction

KDE Plasma 5 is a QT based desktop, that has nearly unlimited customization. It's simple to use but also allows you to change anything you can think off in it's extensive collection of settings. Including Themes, workflow. icons, launchers and anything else you can think off.

It's being developed as part of KDE which is one of the biggest open source communities in the world.

KDE also has it's own Wiki for even more info on their apps https://userbase.kde.org/Welcome_to_KDE_UserBase

Desktop apps

KDE is one of the biggest opensource Eco-systems we will highlights the preinstalled apps but for a full list you can visit https://apps.kde.org/

KDE Connect 

KDE Connect allows you to sync your android phone with your Linux desktop to get notifications, use it as a mouse. Or to simply send commands to your Desktop!

Discover

Discover helps you find and install applications, games, and tools. You can search or browse by category, and look at screenshots and read reviews to help you pick the perfect app.

Dolphin

Dolphin is KDE's file manager that lets you navigate and browse the contents of your hard drives, USB sticks, SD cards, and more. Creating, moving, or deleting files and folders is simple and fast.

KRunner (launched with Alt-F2 or Alt-SPACE) is the launcher built into the Plasma desktop. It can launch programs. switch through tabs, search for folders and files. do calculations and many other things for a full list of features visit https://userbase.kde.org/Plasma/Krunner
